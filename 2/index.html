Good Conventions

  • Underline is a link

  • @something

  • name@something.com

  • google.com

  • Where is the login link?

  • What does clicking the logo do?

  • What does the back button do?

  • What does dragging over text do?

  • Standard names “Contact Us”, “About Us”

  • Extra links - like “Privacy Policy”, “Terms of Use” are where?

Some Bad Conventions

  • Interior Scrollbars

  • No scrollbars

  • Frames

  • Auto-playing media

  • No back button

    User Types

    • Competitive/Assertive

      How will this help me be better? help me win?

    • Spontaneous/Amiable

      What is this in 3 seconds? Why should I use it?

    • Humanistic/Expressive

      Who else is using it? Where's the social proof?

    • Methodical/Analytical

      Where's the scientific proof? Where's the whitepaper?

    Sitemaps

    Why Make Sitemaps?

    • Help you define the content on your site

    • Force you to break down all the pages and sections of your site

    • They help you work out how content will be organized

    • Help you create a better hierarchy
